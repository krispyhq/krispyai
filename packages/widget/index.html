Krispy AI

Live chat with instant AI answers and one-tap human handoff. The bubble is bottom-right — open it and say hi. When the owner replies from Telegram, the AI goes silent and their message appears here live.

Embed on any site (one line)

<script src="https://YOUR-HOST/widget.js"
        data-api="https://krispy-edge.YOU.workers.dev"
        data-tenant="self" async></script>

Attributes: data-api (required, the edge Worker URL), data-tenant (default self), data-title, data-accent.
